About:

A year ago I became interested in level design for video games when I started playing with the map editor included in one of my favorite games. Designing 3d environments is a fun and rewarding hobby that is not limited to games. Recently with online events gaining popularity, designing virtual spaces for platforms like altspaceVR, VRchat, or even Minecraft has become an important means of helping people feel more connected and embodied remotely.
